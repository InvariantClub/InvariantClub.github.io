You've woken up this morning to find a young baby in your care. Can you get them through the day? You will go on adventures, face setbacks, navigate nap coordination and play-group drama; keep them fed and entertained and dressed. Can you get them to sleep so you can get some rest at the end of the day? In this co-operative game you will play with your friends, push your limits, and experience the joys of baby care and fight your way through the trials. Will you navigate the day successfully and (finally) enjoy good night's sleep!?
